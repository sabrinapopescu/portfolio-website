About

Some quick facts about me:

  • I help product teams succeed by setting up frameworks and repeatable systems for design processes. In the last 2 years, I have worked with companies in the e-commerce, education, and mobility industries.
  • I have a background in content marketing. My roles said “marketing”, but my experiences in the startup environment led to multiple collaborations with the product team, made me fall in love with the work and permanently switch to UX/UI design.
  • In my free time, I’m a community coordinator and I help organize the Creative Mornings Amsterdam chapter.
  • Currently reading: Invisible women by Caroline Criado-Perez.

I enjoy working on challenging problems, always in pursuit of learning new skills and exploring new paradigms. My mission is to make the world a better place by empowering people to do what they love and create meaningful connections through design. Though I love all aspects of the design process, I really enjoy working cross-functionally with product and engineering teams to represent the design team and facilitate design discussions.
